How It Works

From cluttered photo to clear decisions in under a minute.

1

Snap a Photo

Point your camera at a cluttered area. The app captures and reviews the image with privacy-first EXIF stripping.

2

AI Groups Your Items

On-device object detection identifies and groups items — cables (5), books (3), clothing (2) — with bounding box previews.

3

Decide in a Sprint

Tap Keep, Donate, Trash, Relocate, or Maybe for each group. The 10-minute timer keeps you focused without overwhelm.

4

Review & Export

See your sprint summary, total resale value, and export everything as a CSV. Celebrate the win.

5

Trade What You Don't Need

List items for local barter trade. The app finds nearby matches, suggests fair credit values, and handles the whole exchange safely.
