# Safety Checklist

## Before Meeting

- [ ] Agreed on price in advance (no surprises)
- [ ] Confirmed meetup time and location
- [ ] Shared listing screenshots showing agreed price (in case they "forget")
- [ ] Charged phone
- [ ] Told a friend/family member: who you're meeting, where, when
- [ ] Shared your live location with someone

## Meetup Location

**Best option:** Police station safe exchange zone
- Long Beach PD has a safe exchange zone in their parking lot
- Most police stations in the LA area have these now

**Backup options:**
- Well-lit public place (shopping center, coffee shop parking lot)
- Near security cameras (bank, grocery store)
- NEVER your home address
- NEVER their home address

## Payment

**Accept ONLY:**
- Cash (bring a counterfeit pen for $100+)
- Zelle (verify funds hit YOUR account before handing over item)
- Venmo (verify funds hit YOUR account before handing over item)

**RED FLAGS — walk away if:**
- They want to use a check
- They mention CashApp, PayPal "Friends & Family," or any other app
- They want to pay more than asking price ("my driver will pick it up")
- They send a "confirmation code" or want you to click a link
- They can't meet in person
- They want to ship it

## E-Bike Specific

- [ ] Hold their car keys OR full cash payment during any test ride
- [ ] Set boundaries: test ride area, time limit (10 min max)
- [ ] Take a photo of their ID (or note their license plate)
- [ ] Remove your lock/key from the bike only after payment clears

## Electronics Specific

- [ ] Power on the item in front of them to confirm it works
- [ ] For phones/tablets: show IMEI, factory reset in front of them
- [ ] For laptops: show it boots, run a quick check
- [ ] Remove your accounts (Apple ID, Google account, etc.) BEFORE handing over

## After the Sale

- [ ] Mark item as sold on all platforms
- [ ] Delete listings from all platforms
- [ ] Note final sale price in tracking.md
- [ ] Leave the buyer a review on OfferUp (they can't see it until they review you)
