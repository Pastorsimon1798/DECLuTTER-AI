## Photos (aim for 8-12)

Order:
1. Best angle / hero shot (first photo — this is the thumbnail)
2. Opposite side / full view
3. Close-up of brand/model label
4. Close-up of any notable features
5. Screen/display powered on (if electronics)
6. Accessories included
7. Original box/manuals (if available)
8. Any minor wear (be honest — builds trust)

Tips:
- Clean the item before photos
- Use good natural light (near a window, no flash)
- Plain background (bedsheet, clean wall)
- No other items in frame
