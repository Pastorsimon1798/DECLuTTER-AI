### Quick Price Check Method

1. Search sold listings on eBay for your exact item
2. Filter to "Sold" — these are actual sale prices
3. Average the last 5-10 sold prices
4. Add 10-15% for local pickup (no shipping fees = perceived value)
5. That's your asking price. List 5-10% above for negotiation room.
