## Getting Started

### Creating Your Account

1. Click **"Sign Up"** on the homepage
2. Choose a **pseudonym** (this protects your identity)
3. Enter your **email** and **password**
4. Optionally add your **location** (helps with local matching)
5. Click **"Create Account"**

**Privacy Note:** MutualCircle uses pseudonyms to protect your privacy. Your real name is never shared unless you choose to share it.

### Navigating the Dashboard

Your dashboard shows:
- **Active Posts:** Your needs/offers currently open
- **Pending Matches:** People who responded to your posts
- **My Pods:** Circles you've joined
- **Upcoming Shifts:** Volunteer commitments
- **Quick Actions:** Fast access to common tasks

## Needs & Offers

### Posting a Need

When you need help from your community:

1. Click **"Post Need/Offer"** or go to **Posts → Create Post**
2. Select **"NEED"** as the type
3. Choose a **category** (Food, Housing, Transportation, etc.)
4. Set **urgency** (Low, Medium, High, Critical)
5. Write a clear **title** and **description**
6. Set your **location** (can be approximate for privacy)
7. Click **"Post"**

**Example:**
- **Title:** "Need ride to doctor appointment"
- **Category:** Transportation
- **Urgency:** Medium
- **Description:** "Looking for a ride to my doctor's appointment on Friday at 2pm. I'm located near downtown and the clinic is on 5th Ave. Happy to contribute gas money!"

### Posting an Offer

When you want to help others:

1. Click **"Post Need/Offer"**
2. Select **"OFFER"** as the type
3. Choose a **category**
4. Write what you're offering
5. Set availability
6. Click **"Post"**

**Example:**
- **Title:** "Offering childcare on weekends"
- **Category:** Childcare
- **Description:** "I'm available for childcare on Saturday and Sunday afternoons. I have experience with kids ages 2-10 and can come to your home."

### Responding to Posts

1. Browse **Posts** page or use **filters** (category, type, urgency)
2. Click on a post that interests you
3. Read the full description
4. Click **"I Can Help"** (for needs) or **"I Need This"** (for offers)
5. Write a brief message explaining how you can help
6. Click **"Send Response"**

### Managing Matches

When someone responds to your post:

1. Go to **Matches** page
2. Review the responses
3. Click **"Accept"** to connect or **"Decline"** to pass
4. Once accepted, you'll receive a **one-time contact token** to exchange details
5. Use the token to share your preferred contact method
6. Mark the match as **"Completed"** when done

**Privacy Tip:** Never share personal information (phone, address) until you've accepted a match.

## Resources Locator

### Finding Resources

MutualCircle helps you find local resources like food pantries, shelters, healthcare, and more.

1. Go to **Resources** page
2. Use the **search bar** or **select a category:**
   - Food Banks & Pantries
   - Shelters & Housing
   - Healthcare Services
   - Financial Assistance
   - Childcare & Education
   - Legal Aid
   - Mental Health
   - Community Services

3. **Filter by:**
   - Distance from you
   - Population served (Seniors, Veterans, Families, etc.)
   - Open now / Open today

4. Click on a resource to see:
   - Address and directions
   - Hours of operation
   - Contact information
   - Services provided
   - Eligibility requirements

### Viewing on the Map

1. Click **"Map View"** toggle
2. See all resources plotted on an interactive map
3. Click markers for details
4. Get directions directly

### Bookmarking Resources

1. Click the **bookmark icon** ⭐ on any resource
2. Access saved resources from **"My Bookmarks"**
3. Remove bookmarks by clicking the icon again

## Volunteer Shifts

### Finding Volunteer Opportunities

1. Go to **Shifts** page
2. View shifts in **Calendar** view (month/week/day)
3. Filter by:
   - Organization
   - Date range
   - Shift type

### Signing Up for a Shift

1. Click on a shift in the calendar
2. Read the shift details:
   - Organization name
   - Date and time
   - Location
   - Description
   - Capacity (spots left)

3. Click **"Sign Up"**
4. You'll receive **confirmation** and **reminders:**
   - 24 hours before: Email + SMS reminder
   - 2 hours before: Final reminder

### Managing Your Shifts

Go to **My Shifts** to:
- See all upcoming commitments
- Cancel a shift (if needed)
- View past shifts history

### For Organizations: Creating Shifts

If you represent a nonprofit:

1. Create an **Organization** profile first
2. Go to **Shifts → Create Shift**
3. Fill in:
   - Title and description
   - Date and time
   - Location
   - Max volunteers needed
   - Requirements (if any)
4. Click **"Create Shift"**

Volunteers will receive automated reminders!

## Pods (Micro-Circles)

Pods are small, close-knit groups (5-20 people) for sustained mutual support.

### Joining a Pod

1. Go to **Pods** page
2. Browse available pods
3. Click on a pod to view details
4. Click **"Join Pod"**
5. Wait for admin approval (if private)

### Creating a Pod

1. Click **"Create Pod"**
2. Choose a **name** and **description**
3. Configure settings:
   - **Privacy:** Public or Private (require approval)
   - **Max Members:** 2-100 (recommended: 5-20)
   - **Check-in Frequency:** How often members check in (1-30 days)
   - **Wellness Alerts:** Enable alerts for missed check-ins
   - **SOS Broadcasts:** Allow emergency broadcasts

4. Click **"Create Pod"** (you become admin)

### Pod Features

#### Wellness Check-Ins

Regular check-ins keep your pod connected:

1. Go to your Pod → Click **"Check In"**
2. Select status:
   - **Doing Well** ✅ (everything is fine)
   - **Need Support** ⚠️ (could use help)
   - **Emergency** 🚨 (urgent assistance needed)

3. Add an optional message
4. Toggle **"Private"** if only admins should see
5. Click **"Submit Check-In"**

Checking in resets your "missed check-ins" counter.

#### SOS Broadcasts

In emergencies, alert all pod members:

1. Go to Pod → Click **"Send SOS"**
2. Choose urgency:
   - **High Priority:** Need help within hours
   - **Critical:** Need immediate assistance

3. Describe what you need
4. Optionally share your location
5. Click **"Send SOS Broadcast"**

**⚠️ Important:** For life-threatening emergencies, call 911 first!

All pod members receive the SOS immediately.

#### Pod Posts

Share updates and communicate internally:

1. Go to Pod → Click **"New Post"**
2. Select post type:
   - **General:** Regular discussion
   - **Announcement:** Important news
   - **Question:** Ask the pod
   - **Resource:** Share helpful info

3. Write title and content
4. Click **"Create Post"**

**Admin Only:** Pin important posts to the top.

#### Managing Members

**For Admins:**
1. Go to Pod → **Members** tab
2. **Invite members** by user ID
3. **Approve/reject** join requests
4. **Change roles** (promote to admin)
5. **Remove members** if needed

#### Wellness Monitoring

**For Admins:**
1. Go to Pod → **Overview** tab
2. View members who missed check-ins
3. See wellness alerts for at-risk members
4. Reach out proactively to support them

## Settings & Privacy

### Updating Your Profile

1. Click your **profile icon** → **Settings**
2. Update:
   - Pseudonym
   - Email
   - Password
   - Location
   - Language preference

### Privacy Controls

**Location Privacy:**
- MutualCircle uses "geohash" technology to approximate your location
- Your exact address is never shared
- You control location precision

**Pseudonym Protection:**
- Your real name is never displayed
- Only accepted matches can exchange contact info

**Check-In Privacy:**
- Toggle "Private" on sensitive check-ins
- Only pod admins see private check-ins

### Notifications

Manage notifications for:
- New matches on your posts
- Shift reminders (24h and 2h before)
- SOS broadcasts from pods
- Check-in reminders
- Pod admin alerts

## Troubleshooting

### Common Issues

**"I can't log in"**
- Reset your password using "Forgot Password"
- Check that you're using the correct email
- Clear browser cache and try again

**"I'm not receiving emails"**
- Check spam folder
- Verify email address in settings
- Add noreply@mutualcircle.org to contacts

**"No resources showing near me"**
- Try expanding search radius
- Check that location is set correctly
- Some categories may have limited availability

**"My post isn't getting responses"**
- Make title clear and specific
- Add more details in description
- Increase urgency if time-sensitive
- Share post link on social media

**"I missed a check-in deadline"**
- Submit a check-in as soon as possible
- It resets your missed counter
- Admins are notified but supportive

### Getting Help

- **Report Issues:** GitHub Issues (link in footer)
- **Email Support:** support@mutualcircle.org

- **FAQ:** mutualcircle.org/faq

## Best Practices

### For Posting Needs

✅ **Do:**
- Be specific about what you need
- Include timeframe (when needed)
- Set appropriate urgency
- Respond to helpers promptly
- Mark completed when done

❌ **Don't:**
- Post vague requests
- Abuse urgency levels
- Ghost people who respond
- Leave posts open indefinitely

### For Offering Help

✅ **Do:**
- Only offer what you can actually provide
- Be reliable and follow through
- Communicate clearly
- Respect boundaries and privacy

❌ **Don't:**
- Overcommit yourself
- Cancel last-minute without notice
- Expect anything in return
- Share others' information

### For Pods

✅ **Do:**
- Check in regularly (builds trust)
- Be supportive and non-judgmental
- Respond to SOS broadcasts if able
- Participate in pod discussions

❌ **Don't:**
- Ignore check-in reminders
- Use SOS for non-emergencies
- Violate pod members' privacy
- Create pods with 50+ people (less effective)

## Safety Tips

1. **Meet in public places** for first-time meetings
2. **Trust your instincts** - decline if something feels off
3. **Don't share financial information** or send money to strangers
4. **Report suspicious behavior** to moderators
5. **Use pseudonyms** until you trust someone
6. **Keep emergency contacts** handy when meeting new people
