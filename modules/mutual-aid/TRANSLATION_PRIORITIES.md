## 🎯 Priority Tier 1: Major World Languages (500M+ speakers)

### 1. Bengali/Bangla 🇧🇩 (`bn`)
- **Global Speakers:** 272M
- **Primary Regions:** Bangladesh, West Bengal (India)
- **Diaspora:** UK (London), Middle East, US
- **Mutual Aid Context:**
  - Bangladesh has extensive grassroots mutual aid networks
  - Frequent natural disasters (cyclones, floods)
  - Strong microfinance and community lending traditions
  - Large diaspora communities with active mutual support
- **Implementation Priority:** ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ **VERY HIGH**

## 📊 Implementation Strategy

### Phase 1: Immediate (Next 3-6 months)
**Focus:** Crisis languages + major gaps
- Ukrainian (40M) - War refugees **URGENT**
- Bengali (272M) - Major gap, disaster-prone
- Indonesian (199M) - 4th most populous country
- Turkish (88M) - Earthquake relief, Syrian refugees

**Impact:** +600M speakers

### Phase 2: High Priority (6-12 months)
**Focus:** Major regional languages + refugee populations
- Persian/Farsi (110M) - Afghan/Iranian refugees
- Punjabi (148M) - Large diaspora
- Hausa (85M) - West African lingua franca
- Swahili (80M L2) - East African lingua franca
- Pashto (40M) - Afghan refugees

**Impact:** +460M speakers

### Phase 3: Medium Priority (12-18 months)
**Focus:** Regional coverage + underserved communities
- Telugu, Marathi (95M each) - South Indian languages
- Polish (45M) - Ukrainian refugee host
- Thai (60M) - SE Asia
- Yoruba, Igbo (95M combined) - Nigerian languages
- Amharic (32M) - Ethiopian crisis

**Impact:** +420M speakers

### Phase 4: Comprehensive Coverage (18-24 months)
**Focus:** Indigenous, minority, and remaining major languages
- Quechua, Guaraní, Nahuatl - Indigenous Americas
- Burmese, Khmer, Lao - Mainland SE Asia
- Somali, Tigrinya - Horn of Africa refugees
- Italian, Romanian - European languages

**Impact:** +200M speakers

---

## 💡 Selection Criteria

Languages are prioritized based on:

1. **Speaker Population** (weight: 30%)
   - Total global speakers
   - L1 vs L2 speakers

2. **Mutual Aid Need** (weight: 30%)
   - Crisis/humanitarian context
   - Community mutual aid traditions
   - Access to existing resources

3. **Digital Access** (weight: 20%)
   - Internet penetration
   - Smartphone adoption
   - Tech literacy

4. **Community Request** (weight: 10%)
   - User feedback
   - Community demand
   - Volunteer translators available

5. **Strategic Impact** (weight: 10%)
   - Regional lingua franca
   - Diaspora reach
   - Platform growth potential
