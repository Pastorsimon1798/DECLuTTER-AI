# 5 Photos to Take Right Now

Wipe the bike down first. Shoot near a window or open garage — no direct sun, no flash.

1. **hero.jpg** — Left side, full bike. Unfolded, squat to bike height, clean wall behind. This is the thumbnail.
2. **display.jpg** — Display powered on. Close enough to read the screen, show 0 miles.
3. **accessories.jpg** — Charger, helmet, lock, fenders, mirror, manual spread out on floor or table in a row.
4. **folded.jpg** — Bike folded in half, photo from the side showing how compact it gets.
5. **motor.jpg** — Rear motor hub. Squat down, zoom in on the back wheel motor.

Save them in `photos/` folder in this project with those exact filenames.
